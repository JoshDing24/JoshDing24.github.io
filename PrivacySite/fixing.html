So before I give you some resources on how to fix your online privacy, I want to preface this by saying that I do not recommend changing everything about your setup all at once. Unfortunately, because companies make more money when you dont have privacy, they try to make it inconvenient for you to have privacy. So all i would recommend doing is looking into the links below and change only a few things at a time until you get used to it. For example, you might decide to start using a vpn more often, or change your browser to one more privacy based.
